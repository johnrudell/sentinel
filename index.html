Sentinel

Sentinel is a web app that pulls data from Google Spreadsheets real time using Tabletop.js. This data represents Genus Holding Co.'s share price, number of shares, principle, earned, and balance, which are then visualized with D3.js.

Genus Holding Co. is an investment club with the purpose of investing the assets of the partnership in stocks, bonds, and projects for education and benefit of the partners.

The eventual goal is to have account holders log in and see their individual standing within the holding company.
